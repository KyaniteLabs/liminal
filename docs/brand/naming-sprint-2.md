# Naming Sprint 2 — Liminal Rebrand

_Date: 2026-04-10_  
_Context: Second naming sprint after rejecting first shortlist (Aftermesh, Chromaforge, Fluxloom, Signalloom, Frameweave) for creative inadequacy._

## Product Context

Creative-coding product for creative technologists, design engineers, artists, experimental makers:
- Code-native visuals (p5, shaders, Three, Hydra, Strudel)
- Not a generic AI app
- Target aesthetic: Figma, Framer, Notion, Resolume, TouchDesigner, Are.na, Vizcom

## Naming Goals

- Beautiful, technical, artistic, modern
- Serious enough to build a company on
- Distinctive and ownable
- Invented or suggestive (not descriptive)
- Easy to say, easy to spell
- Good wordmark/logo potential

## Avoided

- Clunky compounds (Fluxloom-style)
- Generic AI startup vibes
- Obvious words: flux, forge, loom, mesh, signal, frame, nexus, labs, vision, gen, sync, stack
- Corporate/infra-sounding names
- Fantasy/game-style names
